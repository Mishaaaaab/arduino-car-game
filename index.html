ARDUINO CAR GAME

A simple Arduino game controlled by two buttons. Move the car up or down to avoid obstacles. Collision ends the game.

Platform: Arduino
Input: 2 buttons
Display: LCD 1602

Overview

This page presents my Arduino project: Arduino Car Game. The goal is to practice embedded programming, input handling, and simple game state logic.
