Overview

Arduino Car Game is a simple Arduino-based game controlled by two buttons. The player moves a car up or down to avoid obstacles displayed on an LCD screen.

  • Two-button control
  • LCD 1602 display
  • Obstacle avoidance gameplay
  • Game Over on collision
